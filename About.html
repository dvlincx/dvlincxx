About Simply, Coffee.

Simply, Coffee is a Cafe that specializes in Coffee. It offers many things such as their famous coffee, salads, sandwiches, cookies and also offers things that can be also found in buffets such as the main course and side dishes. Simply, Coffee is an eco-friendly cafe that lessens the usage of plastic and encourages citizens to buy reusable products and so does the cafe! For instance, most cafe uses plastic while Simply, Coffee enhances the usage of non-plastic material. Other cafe uses straws while this cafe is strawless. You can also ask one of our fellow managers instead of using a cardboard cup, you can give them your reusable bottle!
